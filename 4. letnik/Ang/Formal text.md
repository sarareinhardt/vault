##### Formal email/letter
A formal letter or email is factional, impersonal -- just reveals certain demanded facts about you. It is short and explanatory
Starts with dear sir or madam/dear Mr Smith (if we have both a name and surname we only use the surname) Mr pa Ms je brez pike
Ends with i look forward to your reply, **yours faithfully** 

**You need to devide the letter in paragraphs !!! ** 

Introduction: short max 3 sentences. We write the purpose of our writing and it needs to be clear who we are (that can either be our name or we can say for example "as a participant of your course) and who you are writing to - in this case you explain that when you say that you attended the course. Who you are, why are you writing, who are you writing to

In the next paragraph you explain what happend (to the certificate) *to si zmisliš* 
In the 3rd paragraph you say why you need the copy
	these paragraphs should also be short without any emotional parts (don't say i'm sorry)
Conclusion: we thank the person in advance, we summarize what we want

I look forward to your reply/to hearing from you/to seeing you/...
**Yours, faithfully,/Yours, sincerely,** (ne pisat kind regards ali kaj podobnega sam eno od teh 2)

No shortening the words - don't is wrong - do not is right

**Če napišeš da si videl oglas tabiš napisat kje si videl oglas** - vir (ne vem zakaj)


##### Report
Each paragraph needs a title including introduction!!! (it needs to be clear that that is a title), the last title is conclusion and very often you have a recommendation paragraph where you recommend improvements
We always start the title with Report on ...
1. **Report on...** (Report on our excursion)
2. Introduction - expresses the purpose of the report and what the report is about, who you are, who required it, what details you are going to reveal (time, place...), method of obtaining the information questionnaire... and how many people participated in it
3. Usually about 2 - 3 paragraphs with titles
4. Conclusion - sum up the results and findings
5. (Recommendations) you recommend improvements

Factional
All reports are based on some kind of research work
