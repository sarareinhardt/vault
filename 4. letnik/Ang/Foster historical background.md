##### The history of fostering in ireland
- important element of irish history and legeng
- **the brehon laws**
	- fees paid to the foster parents (depending on the gender and status of the child) 
	- strict rules regarding the treatment of the children
	- foster parents took legal liability during this time
	- if something happens to the child the foster parents had to pay compensations to the biological parents
- Children were raised outsoide their own families as they thought this would build strong allinces (if your child was fosterd by another clan you probably wouldn't want to go into battle with them or woud be less likely to)
- encourage **solutions to disputes** outside of battles
- **Kinship care** = when family or friends take over care of a child because the biological parents aren't able to because of some sort of crisis. Another expression is relative care
